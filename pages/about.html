Our Story

Built to
deliver

Athera Technologies was founded in 2024 in Lithuania by engineers who spent years working inside multinational enterprises. We've seen what works and what doesn't. We know that technology projects fail not because of the code, but because of unclear requirements, changing priorities, and teams that don't truly understand the business.

Our team brings enterprise-grade experience deploying automation, data infrastructure, security solutions, and custom software across complex organisations. We've operated in regulated industries, managed systems at scale, and navigated the political dynamics of bringing change into large companies. We understand what it takes to execute.

We're small enough to move fast, but experienced enough to anticipate the obstacles that trip up larger firms. When we commit to a project, we deliver — on scope, on timeline, and with the discipline that enterprise clients expect.
